FAQ

User

Can I convert subscription funds to one-time charge funds?

One-time charges and subscriptions founds are separated. However, depending on application policy, you can ask the developer to credit your one-time charge funds.

Developer

Why would I use MonetizeJS in my application?

Because:

  • You are developing a pure client side application, so you don’t have any server,
  • Or you have web services but you don’t want to manage users and payment transactions on your server.

How much does it cost?

MonetizeJS applies a 5% fee on top of the Stripe fees (2.4% + 24c + VAT per successful charge, for more information: https://stripe.com/pricing).
